Parking Info

For ease of parking there are 3 multi-storey carparks located near our address: Bullring car park, Selfridges and Moor Street car park, for a car park out the way and less busy we recommend Digbeth public car park (7 min walk).

On bank holidays and weekends parking can usually be busy, so we recommend Digbeth car park. It is a 7 minute walk away, follow the footpath up oxford street (signposted) to the main road where you will take a right and follow that straight up towards the bullring building.
