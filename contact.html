Parking info

For ease of parking there are 3 mlti-storey carparks located near our address, Bullring car park with enterance just of the main road that is signposted with a space availability board also stood outside, Selfridges and moor street car park on the right hand side also with space availability signs that are viewable from the road, to access resturant from this car park proceed to the 3rd floor and take the sky bridge over to selfridges and use the lifts located on the right hand side to floor 0, upon exit take the door on the left and we are located straight in front of you. For bank holidays and weekends parking can be busy so we would recommend using the 3rd car park which is Digbeth public car park located a 5 min walk away just off Oxford street.
